Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 31, 2025

1. Introduction

LaborLooker ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our contractor matching and referral platform.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Business information for contractors
  • Billing and payment information
  • Profile photos and business documentation

Usage Information

  • Platform activity and feature usage
  • Communication preferences and history
  • Search queries and contractor interactions
  • Device information and IP addresses

3. How We Use Your Information

Essential Platform Functions (Required)

  • Service Delivery: Matching contractors with customers and facilitating connections
  • Payment Processing: Handling transactions, commissions, and billing
  • Safety and Security: Identity verification, fraud prevention, and platform security
  • Legal Compliance: Contract management, dispute resolution, and regulatory requirements
  • Customer Support: Responding to inquiries and resolving issues

Enhanced Features (With Your Consent)

  • Marketing Communications: Personalized job alerts, contractor recommendations, and platform updates
  • Advanced Personalization: AI-powered matching, customized dashboard, and smart recommendations
  • Performance Analytics: Platform optimization, bug fixes, and user experience improvements
  • Market Research: Anonymized insights for platform development and industry analysis

Your Choice Matters: Optional features require your explicit consent and can be disabled anytime. Declining these features won't prevent you from using LaborLooker's core services.

4. Data Protection and PII Masking

We implement comprehensive privacy protection measures:

PII Protection Features

  • Email Masking: Display only domain (@domain.com)
  • Phone Number Masking: Show only last 4 digits (***-1234)
  • Name Masking: Display first name + last initial (John S.)
  • Address Masking: Show only city and state

Privacy Controls

  • Granular privacy settings for profile visibility
  • Monthly profile view limits for enhanced protection
  • Comprehensive audit trail of data access
  • User-controlled data retention settings

Manage your privacy settings: Privacy Dashboard

5. Your Privacy Rights

GDPR Rights (EU Residents)

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain data processing

CCPA Rights (California Residents)

  • Right to Know: Information about data collection
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

Exercise Your Rights: Visit our Privacy Settings or contact privacy@laborlooker.com

6. Data Security

We protect your information through:

  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Role-based access with authentication
  • Security Headers: Enhanced Content Security Policy protection
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and compliance reviews
  • Incident Response: Established breach notification procedures

7. Data Sharing and Third Parties

Legitimate Business Sharing

We may share limited information with:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, payment processing, email services (with data processing agreements)
  • Platform Users: Verified contractor-customer information for service delivery (with appropriate masking)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or legal process
  • Safety and Security: To prevent fraud, protect users, and ensure platform integrity

Marketing and Research (With Your Consent)

  • Marketing Partners: Anonymized demographics for relevant advertising (opt-in only)
  • Research Organizations: Aggregated industry insights and market trends (non-identifying data)
  • Platform Integrations: Third-party tools and services to enhance your experience (with explicit consent)

We do not sell personal information to third parties. Any data sharing for marketing or research purposes requires your explicit consent and uses anonymized/aggregated data only.

Revenue Model Transparency

LaborLooker generates revenue through:

  • Platform Fees: Transaction fees on completed projects (10% + 5% service fee)
  • Network Referral Fees: 5% fee for successful network referrals
  • Premium Features: Enhanced profiles, priority placement, advanced analytics
  • Advertising: Sponsored content and featured listings (clearly marked)

10. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

10. International Users

Our services are primarily designed for users in the United States. International users should be aware that:

  • Data may be transferred to and processed in the United States
  • Local privacy laws may provide additional protections
  • We comply with applicable international privacy regulations
  • Users may have additional rights under local law

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will take immediate steps to delete the information.

12. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Material changes will be communicated through:

  • Email notification to registered users

We provide 30 days' notice for significant changes affecting your rights.
