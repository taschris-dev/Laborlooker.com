Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started on LaborLooker?

Getting started is easy! Simply register for an account, select your account type (Professional/Contractor, Customer, or Job Seeker), complete your profile, and start connecting with opportunities in your area.

What is the commission structure?

LaborLooker charges a 10% commission on successful job connections. This helps us maintain and improve the platform while connecting skilled professionals with local opportunities.

How do I update my privacy settings?

You can manage your privacy preferences at any time by visiting your Privacy Settings page. You can control what information is shared, manage consents, and export or delete your data.

How does the swipe matching system work?

Our swipe system helps you discover opportunities quickly. Swipe right on jobs or contractors you're interested in, left to pass. When both parties show interest, it's a match and you can connect directly!

What payment methods do you accept?

We integrate with PayPal for secure payment processing. You can pay invoices directly through the platform using PayPal or any major credit/debit card.
